Background

The National Skills Reform Agenda is about improving the VET sector to ensure Australia has a highly skilled workforce ready for the job market, now and into the future.

These reforms are the next step in improving the VET system and continuing the work undertaken through reviews and consultations over the past 18 months.

The VET sector supports and develops diverse skills across a broad range of industries. A strong VET sector is vital to the Australian economy. Reforms will strengthen the training system and support Australia's immediate recovery after the impacts of COVID-19.

The feedback we receive will be considered by Skills Ministers to inform decisions on future arrangements in the VET system.

You can find out more about Skills Reform in our Frequently Asked Questions page.
